Chang's Chicken Prep

  1. Weigh out 12 oz of sliced chicken.
  2. Coat the chicken in starch mix.
  3. Cook chicken in fryer for 2-3 minutes or until no pink.
  4. Heat your wok until lightly smoking.
  5. Add 1 tb of oil in a circular motion.
  6. Cook your aromatics for 10 seconds.
  7. Add 3 fl oz of White Sauce and 4 fl oz of Chang's Sauce
  8. When boiling, add 1 tb of Corn Starch Slurry

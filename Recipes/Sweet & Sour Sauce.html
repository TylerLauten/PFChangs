Sweet & Sour Sauce Recipe

Ingredients needed

  • Vinegar - 4 Quarts (1 gallon)
  • Water - 5 Quarts (1.25 gallon)
  • Ketchup - 1 Bag (tbd amount)
  • Sugar - 4 Quarts (1 gallon)
  1. Combine the Vinegar and Water together into a 22qt cambro
  2. Add the Ketchup and Sugar and stir until completely dissolved
  3. Cover and refrigerate for 24 hours

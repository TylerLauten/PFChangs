Sweet & Sour Cooking Prep

  1. Weigh out six ounces of sliced chicken.
  2. Coat the chicken in starch mix.
  3. Pour 4 oz of batter onto plate
  4. Thoroughly combine and place chicken into fryer piece by piece.
  5. Let the chicken cook for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown
  6. Add vegetables to fryer basket for 10 seconds
  7. Remove from oil and place on your spider.
  8. Heat 6 fl oz of []Sweet&Sour Sauce to a boil
  9. Add 2 tb of []Cornstarch Slurry
